Application Agreement
  • By submitting this form and signing my name below, I certify that the information provided is true and correct. United Doberman Rescue reserves the right to refuse adoption of any Doberman/dog in its program for any reason.
  • I understand there is a $20.00 non-refundable application fee to submit this application, which will be applied to the adoption fee only if I am approved to adopt.
  • I understand filling out the application and paying the fee does not mean I will automatically be approved to adopt.
  • I have read all adoption requirements before filling out this application and agree I qualify to adopt a dog from UDR.
  • I understand UDR will contact my vet to ensure my current and past pets are fully vetted and current on vaccines, are on heart worm preventative and are all spayed and neutered. Failure to give accurate vet info will close my application.
  • I agree that the Doberman/dog I adopt will not be used for breeding purposes, and will be altered by 6 months of age if adopted when a puppy. There is no exception to this rule.
  • I agree that if I cannot keep the Doberman/dog for any reason, I will return it to United Doberman Rescue immediately, forfeiting any adoption donation.
  • I give the right to United Doberman Rescue and Canine Castaways of Minnesota (“UDR”) to contact and obtain information from my current and past vets, and any references listed to verify my suitability as a dog owner.
  • All information provided to UDR volunteers will be held confidential and may not be used or shared for any other purpose without my permission.
  • I understand UDR is 100% volunteer and I will stay in contact and respond to all calls and emails from the volunteers who reach out to me. Failure to provide an accurate email or phone number, or respond in a timely manner to the UDR volunteers will close my application.
  • I hereby release from liability, UDR and its representative from seeking, gathering and using such information, and all other persons, corporations or organizations for furnishing such information.

Upon submitting this form, you will be redirected to our "Online Store" to pay a $20 NON-Refundable application fee, which will be applied to the adoption fee should you adopt. If you do not adopt, are denied, or adopt elsewhere, this $20 application fee is considered a donation and will not be returned. Once you are in the online store, please select the "Adoption Application Fee" product, put it in your shopping cart, and check out through PayPal.
